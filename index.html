All Glory to God in the Highest

  • Genesis 1:9-13 (ESV)
  • Psalm 96:11-13 (ESV)
  • Psalm 19:1-6 (ESV)

The unequivocal non-verbal declaration

It's abundantly evident from the Scriptural testimony that all of creation would cry out in praise if they but had a voice.

Creation Glory Examples

The waters were gathered together by sheer omnipotence exercised ex nihilo.

The field exults anticipating the foreshadowed righteousness of the Lord.

The heavens declare the glory of God and the firmament shows His handiwork!

He answered, “I tell you, if these were silent, the very stones would cry out.”

- Luke 19:40
